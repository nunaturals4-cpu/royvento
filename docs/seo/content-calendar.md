# Royvento тАФ 90-Day Content Calendar

This calendar maps 12 weeks of blog publishing aligned to the Indian nightlife/event calendar. Each week ships **1 cornerstone city/locality post + 1 supporting funnel post**, plus a paired social cut. Internal links point back to programmatic templates (city pages, locality pages, offer pages) so each blog feeds the booking funnel.

> **Cadence**: 2 posts/week (Tue + Fri). Republish/refresh seasonal posts annually instead of writing new ones each year.
> **Owner mix**: Content lead writes; partners contribute 1 quote/photo per piece for E-E-A-T; ops verifies offers/timings before publish.
> **Each post must include**: 1 H1, scannable H2/H3s, FAQ block (FAQPage schema), 3+ internal links, 1 outbound link to a credible local source, hero image with locality ALT, author bio.

## Indian seasonality anchors used below
- **AugтАУSep**: Monsoon weekends, Independence Day long weekend, Ganesh Chaturthi (Maharashtra/Karnataka).
- **Oct**: Navratri (dry-day caveats in Gujarat), Halloween, IPL playoffs/Premier League start.
- **Nov**: Diwali week (dry days vary by city), wedding-season kickoff, F1 watch parties.
- **Dec**: Christmas, NYE peak (single-biggest revenue weekend of the year for pubs).
- **Jan**: NYE recap, Republic Day long weekend, college fest season begins.
- **Feb**: Valentine's, college fest peak, Six Nations / cricket series.
- **MarтАУMay**: IPL season (every weekend = sports-bar gold), Holi (Mar), end-of-financial-year corporate offsites, summer nights.

## Always-on (publish opportunistically alongside calendar)
- **City announcements**: when a new city goes live, publish "Royvento launches in {city}: 10 pubs to book first."
- **Partner launches**: when a flagship pub onboards, publish "{Pub Name} now bookable on Royvento" with vendor quote.
- **Offer alerts**: monthly "Pub offers in {city} this month" refreshes тАФ same URL, updated content (good for freshness signal).

## Internal-link rules
1. Every blog must link to **(a)** a city hub, **(b)** a locality or category page, and **(c)** at least one specific pub or event detail page.
2. NYE/seasonal hubs must link out to all city variants and back up to the parent occasion hub.
3. Programmatic pages link **into** blogs via a "Related reads" rail (helps blog indexation).
